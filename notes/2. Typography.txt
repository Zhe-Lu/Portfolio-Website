The Purpose of a Font:
- Easy to read
- Communitcate Values and tone (aesthetics)

Serif vs Sans-serif
- Serif has a bit of decoration while sans-serif is a cleaner typeface
- Sans-serif has a more modern and clean look
- Seriff has an older look thats premium/important/established look

You do not want long lines make makes the user tilt/move thier head to read
This reduces the legibility of the text and it is more uncomfortable to read
- make sure that each line is about 10 words

Suggested Serif Fonts:
- Garamond
- Bodoni
- Century Expanded
- Times Roman

Suggested Sans-Seriff Fonts:
- Futura
- Helvetica
